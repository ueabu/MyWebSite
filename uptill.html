UpTill App

UpTill is an app that helps you keep track and countdown to events you have coming up. It shows you the number of days left till your event. You can also customize the countdown by adding background pictures to the countdown. When you tap a countdown, it shows you a live countdown, to the second, of the date and time of your event. See screenshots below.

Motivation

It had been a while since I worked on a mobile app and I wanted to work on one that was not to complicated.
