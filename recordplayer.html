Modern Day Record Player

Inspired by Talaexe on TikTok, I came up with a solution to automate my morning routine by using RFID cards to play specific playlist from spotify. See this video for more information

Motivation

I wanted to work on a second project that involved a raspberry pi and also work with the spotify API.
